Professional Work

My work experiences have allowed me to take on projects of all sorts. I have extensive experience in journalistic and client-related writing. Below are just two of the many projects I have completed in my career.

Byline: Higher Education

While at Cookerly Public Relations, I was given the opportunity to write a byline on behalf of a higher education client. The byline, which goes to publication in early 2020, required intensive research, attention to detail and communication with the client. From conception to completion, I was given the responsibility of writing the byline and seeing it through. The fact that the agency placed their trust in me to deliver on this task is a demonstration of my writing prowess.

Journalism: Chefs in their essence

As an intern in the food and drink department at Creative Loafing, I was given the opportunity to interview renowned photographer Erik Tanner about his exhibition of portraits of regional chefs:

Kicking off this past weekend, Ponce City Market hosts Atlanta Gallery Collective, a pop-up art exhibition celebrating some of the formative members of Atlanta's food scene. The flagship exhibit, a series of portrait photographs captured by Brooklyn-based photographer Erik Tanner, features James Beard Award-winning and nominated chefs and mixologists from the Southeast.
